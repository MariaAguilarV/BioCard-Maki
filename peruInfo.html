I am from Peru, a South American country rich in gastronomy, adventure, culture an nature. I have born and grown up in Cusco, a city located in the southeastern of Peru. Cusco shows the county’s natural and cultural heritage at its finest, here lies Machu Picchu, the most visited natural protected area in Peru. I have been there only once, which makes me think that I have to make another visit soon!
In 2009, after graduating from high school, I moved to Lima, the capital city, to start the University. Lima’s culinary traditions are the fruit of the widespread intermingling of cultures – from Peru and many other countries. These traditions, when combined with the ingredients of a country as biodiverse as Peru, have given rise to cuisine that won it Best Culinary Destination at the 2017 World Travel Awards.
